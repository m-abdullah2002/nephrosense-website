NephroSense – Smarter Kidney Care

AI-Based Kidney Disease Prediction

Empowering Doctors. Reaching Communities.

NephroSense is an AI-powered diagnostic tool that helps healthcare professionals detect Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) at early stages using machine learning, SHAP explainability, and federated learning. Designed with underserved regions in mind.

  • Contact Us

Key Features

CKD Stage Prediction

Predicts chronic kidney disease stages with high accuracy using clinical lab test data.

Explainable AI (SHAP)

Doctors can understand how each medical parameter contributed to a patient's prediction.

Federated Learning

Enables decentralized learning on Raspberry Pi devices without sharing patient data.

Pakistan-Focused

Built for local healthcare needs. Lightweight, accessible, and targeted for remote clinics.

About NephroSense

We're a student-led startup from Iqra University aiming to make AI healthcare solutions accessible across Pakistan. NephroSense predicts CKD stages early and supports community clinics and hospitals.
